Patrick Stoica

Software developer and ambient musician

From Atlanta, based in NYC

I graduated from Georgia Tech with a B.S. in Computer Science. Previously, I dabbled in web development and programming through middle and high school.

My professional experience includes Squarespace, Yahoo! Sports, and the occasional freelance gig. My expertise is in full-stack development, with an emphasis on frontend.

Lately my interests are expanding to the creative, educational, and political aspects of programming and computation. I make electronic and ambient music and have begun incorporating tools like Tidal, SuperCollider, and Web Audio. I'm currently a volunteer with ScriptEd.

I'm also a WREK alumnus and produced weekly shows on Live at WREK.

P.S. I'm currently looking for work!
